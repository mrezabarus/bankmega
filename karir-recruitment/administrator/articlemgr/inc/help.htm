    Inserts a Table into your document. You are given the following options.
    • Rows: The number of rows you want your table to have.
    • Columns: The number of columns you want your table to have.
    • Padding: The space of the border to the cell content on your table.
    • Spacing: The space of the border to another border on your table.
    • Width: The table width.
    • Border: The thickness of the border on your table. 1 usually looks good.
    Once your table is inserted into your document you can click on the table with your right mouse button for more options.
    • Insert Row: Inserts a row into the table.
    • Delete Rows: Deletes a row.
    • Insert Column: Inserts a column into the table.
    • Delete Columns: Deletes a column.
    • Insert Cell: Inserts a table cell into the table. (one box)
    • Delete Cells: Deletes a table cell. (one box)
    • Merge Cells: Transforms two cells into one.
    • Split Cells: Takes one cell and makes two.
    • Edit Table: Edit properties of the table
